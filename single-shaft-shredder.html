The Single Shaft Shredder is designed for efficient recycling of polymer materials.

  • Energy efficient: Paired with an optimized grinder/granulator, it significantly reduces energy consumption.
  • V-CUT Technology: Offers precise cuts with minimal power usage and stress on materials.
  • Hydraulic PUSH System: Ensures smooth operation and material handling.
  • Special Gearbox Design: Reduces motor power requirements and energy consumption.
  • Easy Maintenance: Simple access for cleaning and part replacement.
  • Versatile Operation: Functions with or without a screen for different chip sizes.
  • Efficient Maintenance: Quick access to rotor and knives for easy maintenance.
  • Plastics: Includes various rigid and flexible plastics like mouldings, purgings/lumps, profiles, and films.
  • Mixed Waste: RDF/SRF production from household and commercial waste.
  • Timber/Wood: Processing pallets, joiners waste, and green waste.
  • Foam: Production waste management.
  • Paper & Cardboard: Confidential documents, packaging materials, and production waste.
  • Rubber: Skeletal scrap and production waste.
  • Non-Ferrous Metals: Various applications.
  • Textiles: Processing carpets (rolls & tiles), garments, etc.
  • Packaging Materials: Includes cardboard.
  • Aluminium: Used beverage cans, swarf, and cables.
  • Copper Cable: Household, telecoms, and industrial cables.
  • Security Destruction: Handling counterfeit items, faulty goods, and outdated stock.
